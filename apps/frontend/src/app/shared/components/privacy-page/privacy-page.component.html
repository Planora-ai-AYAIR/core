01 Introduction

At Planora, we respect the privacy of our business users. This Privacy Policy details how Planora Egypt collects, processes, protects, and stores corporate or personal details when utilizing our spatial visualization and B2B mapping framework.

02 Information We Collect

Because Planora is currently an active, free Minimum Viable Product (MVP), we minimize our data perimeter. We collect the following datasets to sustain normal platform operations:

  • Account Profiles: Corporate business emails, passwords, usernames, and designated system roles (Client/Admin).
  • Spatial Metadata & Assets: Geographic coordinates, geometric boundary layouts, and custom parcel information files uploaded by clients to run spatial calculations.
  • Technical Logs: Device IP addresses, browser variants, and core interaction records compiled automatically during your active session.

03 How Your Data Is Utilized

Your corporate operational logs and geospatial files are processed strictly for specific engineering targets:

  • To provision, isolate, and authenticate secure B2B client dashboard domains.
  • To run mapping calculations, execute parcel analyses, and output PDF/visual planning logs.
  • To trace software issues, prevent unauthorized mapping scrapers, and upgrade the MVP engine stability.

Strict Third-Party Sharing Rules

Planora does not trade, sell, or distribute your proprietary parcel parameters or account profiles to commercial third parties or external data warehouses. Your configurations remain securely tied to your system profile workspace.

05 Data Security & Regional Hosting

We execute modern administrative encryption tokens to guard access pathways. In line with local regulatory data provisions within Egypt, all server infrastructure stacks, relational user indexes, and uploaded spatial vectors are maintained inside reliable cloud structures protected by modern password-hashing techniques and TLS/SSL encryption blocks during transits.

06 Your Legal Rights (Egypt Law No. 151 of 2020)

Under the Egyptian Data Protection framework, verified business contact representatives preserve explicit organizational privileges regarding their data profiles:

Right to Access & Edit

You may inspect or update administrative account profiles through your internal client settings panel at any time.

Right to Erasure

You can formally request complete elimination of your profile assets, parsing sequences, and boundary markers when closing corporate partnerships.

07 Contact Channels

If your firm holds questions regarding processing mechanics, coordinate safety practices, or wishes to request complete platform database removal, please open a communication ticket with our system administration team at support@planora.com.
